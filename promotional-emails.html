Promotional Emails

The term 'promotional emails' says it all. It is used for communicating special offers or promotions to your customers. In contrast to transactional emails, promotional emails may not have as high open rates. Email marketing is believed to have one of the highest success rates, but when it comes to promotional content, your brand strategy needs to be balanced and synced thoroughly with your organizational goals.

  • Should be sent only to numbers that have a distinct 'Do No Disturb' setting.
  • Should be sent to numbers that are opt-in numbers
  • Need to be sent during fixed duration - it is usually 0900 hours to 2100 hours.
  • Promotional SMS is sent as a 6-digit number that is a random sender ID as a mandatory rule.
