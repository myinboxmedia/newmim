SMTP Relay

This service is like one more feather in our cap. With the help of this service, one can send marketing emails and transactional emails to the entire mailing list without needing to support own email server.

What this service provides?

  • Real-time email tracking with advanced analytics. Detailed logs to diagnose email failure and delivery. Increased deliverability features to reduce bounce rates.
  • Getting your SMTP service set up for transactional emails and email marketing is trouble-free with MiM , all you need to do is insert your SMTP credentials into your application, and we take care of the rest.
