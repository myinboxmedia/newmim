Transactional Emails

Transactional emails are a form of communication that briefs about a particular transaction - it could be regarding a confirmation, delivery information, receipts, account creation, notifications, password resets, One-Time password, alerts related to bookings and orders, and more. This form of email has comparatively higher open rates.

Since transactional emails are about business - these carry specific information, one that the customer has asked for or one that would assist the customer in completing a transaction with your brand - such emails need to be flawlessly delivered to the right person, at the right time. For example, when a user responds to an account email creation, it lets your brand connect with them and is an incredible opportunity to exhibit your brand strengths.

My Inbox Media, India's premier bulks SMS company, has designed a top-notch automated transactional email system that helps create fast, integrated and responsive templates. With a strong and robust backend, use our system because:

  • Versatile API setup with SMTP for integration
  • Dedicated IP address
  • Email authentication
  • Real-time tracking and monitoring
  • Powerful analytics
  • Customized reporting to determine user interaction over a period.
  • Accessible round the clock
  • Scalable, flexible, agile, and adaptable
  • Assists with reputation monitoring.

The significance of transactional emails leaves a telltale impact on your email marketing strategy. With My Inbox Media, you have a trusted service provider with Cloud technology and mass SMS service capabilities to offer an unparalleled support framework. MiM is committed to invent and innovate automated technologies that make new-age brand communication top niche and high performing.
