Cloud Telephony

Cloud telephony, also known as cloud Voice Solution, is a type of unified communications as a service that offers voice communication services through a third-party host.

UCaaS replaces the need for traditional enterprise telephone systems, such as private branch exchange. Cloud telephony services can be web- or applications-based and can be accessed from anywhere over the internet.

Benefits of Cloud telephony:
  • Cost-efficient solution for Small business and large enterprises.
  • Flexible infrastructure helps in integrations with third party apps.
  • Pay on The Go as per your usage.
  • Scalable with a click.

Types of Services:

IVR :

Talk | Track | Record | Analyse

Best for call centres and lead generation purpose, this service comes with flexible plans and agents management.

Toll Free:

Don’t Let your Customer Pay | Talk | Track | Record | Analyse

Best for Brands who wanted to give their customers super privileged feel while interacting with them. This platform can handle large number of incoming calls with ease.

Miss Call:

Track | Analyse

Best to use for capturing the consent from your customers, this facility is used by many brands for customer Opt-in’s.

OBD:

Auto Dial | Record | Track | Analyse

One Voice message to all, send Jingles, promotional content, informative content or the CEO’s/Brand Ambassadors voice message to the customers.

Voice API:

Automate Dialling | Record | Track | Analyse

Integrate with any Third-party Application to automate the outbound calls. Hook URL’s are available to fetch the reports in your depository.
