Link Tracking

Stay updated in real-time on the progress of your bulk SMS campaigns with My Inbox Media. Get detailed reports of click-through rates on your URL with the Link Tracking feature.

My Inbox Media's Link Tracker:

  • Helps convert Long URL to Short Links. The links can be customized to make them look impressive and attract customer attention.
  • Helps you get details of potential customers who have clicked on your link, sent through mass SMS service - for promotional and transactional SMSes.
  • Get details of the phone numbers that have visited the link, the time of visit, the number of visits, browser details, location details, and other technical details - all customized to meet your specific requirement.
  • Run your SMS campaigns with the powerful Link Tracker tool from MiM made with the latest technology; empowered to offer detailed insight into your potential customer profile and behaviour. A must-have analytical tool to drive results and revenues!

How does our Link Tracker work?

  • The tracker first converts long links to short URLs by generating a unique short token that is just about 6 to 8 characters long.
  • The bulk SMS is then sent to recipients with the short link embedded within the SMS content.
  • When they click on the clink, the customer is redirected to your website link.

An automated way to communicating with your prospective customers - let it be any content or campaign. Send volume SMS with India's top bulk SMS service provider and get the competitive advantage of hi-tech tools that facilitate live tracking and measuring of campaign productivity.
